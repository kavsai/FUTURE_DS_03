# 📊 Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes marketing campaign performance using the Bank Marketing dataset. The objective is to evaluate customer conversion rates, identify high-performing customer segments, analyze campaign effectiveness, and provide actionable business recommendations through an interactive Power BI dashboard.

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze customer conversion performance.
- Measure overall conversion rate.
- Identify high-converting customer segments.
- Compare conversions across job, education, marital status, contact method, and month.
- Generate business insights and recommendations.

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop
- Microsoft Excel / CSV
- GitHub

## 📂 Dataset
**Dataset:** Bank Marketing Dataset

The dataset contains customer information from direct marketing campaigns conducted by a Portuguese banking institution.

## 📊 Dashboard Features
- Total Customers
- Converted Customers
- Conversion Rate
- Average Age
- Average Campaign Contacts
- Customer Conversion Funnel
- Conversions by Job
- Conversions by Education
- Conversions by Marital Status
- Conversions by Month
- Contact Method Distribution
- Interactive Slicers

## 📈 Key Insights
- The overall customer conversion rate is approximately 12%.
- Management and Technician customers recorded the highest conversions.
- Customers with Secondary and Tertiary education converted more frequently.
- Married customers accounted for the highest number of successful conversions.
- Cellular contact was the most effective communication method.
- Conversion performance varied across different months.

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Focus marketing campaigns on high-converting customer segments.
- Increase campaign efforts during high-performing months.
- Prioritize cellular contact for customer outreach.
- Personalize campaigns based on customer demographics.
- Continuously monitor KPIs to improve marketing performance.
